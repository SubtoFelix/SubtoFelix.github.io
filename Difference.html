Comparing between Good and Bad Comics/Animations

Excellent Poor
Plot The story has a clear beginning and background setting. It also doesn't deviate from the main plot. The story has no clear goal and is all over the place.
Pacing The flow of the story is not too fast and not too slow. It should contain a balance of actions and moments of reflection. Everything is either going too fast that the viewers cannot catch up or too slow that it becomes boring.
Characters Characters have clear motives and flaws. They go through character development and are distinct from one another. Characters are bland and don't have any goals. They stay the same throughout the whole plotline.
Colours Contrast The colours convey emotions like joy, sadness, anger, and fear. The contrast create a calm and dramatic effect in their corresponding scenes. The colours are bland and the same throughout the whole story. Different scenes don't contain distinct colours that represent their intended purposes.
Examples Kiki's Delivery Service, Seasons of Blossom, Your Letter A Troll in Central Park, Alpha and Omega
**The Examples are subjective**
